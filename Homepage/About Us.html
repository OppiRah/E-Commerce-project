Our Story

Founded in 2024, BuyMangasPH was born from a passion for bringing the best of East Asian storytelling to Filipino readers. We recognized the growing demand for authentic manga, manhwa, and manhua in the Philippines and set out to create a platform that celebrates these incredible art forms.

What started as a small collection has grown into the Philippines' premier destination for East Asian comics, serving thousands of readers nationwide.

Our Mission

To make authentic East Asian comics accessible to every Filipino reader, fostering a vibrant community that appreciates diverse storytelling traditions from Japan, Korea, and China.

We believe in the power of stories to connect cultures, inspire imagination, and bring people together through shared experiences.
